Bolivia

Recently, I took a trip to the world's largest salt flat, located in Uyuni, Bolivia.


Fun facts about this salt flat:

  • It stretches across 10,582 kilometers.
  • It has an estimated total of 10 billion tonnes of salt.
  • Approximately 70% of the world's lithium reserves can be found beneath this salt flat.
